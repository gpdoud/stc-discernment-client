Committee caller instructions:

Committee members should call each parishioner assigned to tell them that they have been recommended for discernment. You should try calling up to three times so you can talk to them. If after the third call, you don't get a call back, you should send them an email.

Before you talk to a parishioner, you should be familiar with the Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) and the Script. Once you explain the discernment process and answer any questions, you should ask them directly if they would participate in the process. The possible responses are:

  • Yes - I will attend BOTH sessions March 13 & 27
  • Next Year - I cannot attend BOTH sessions this year. Call me next year.
  • No - I do not want to participate in discernment.

Circle the appropriate response on the Committee Call list. Once you have responses from all parishioners assigned, send an email to Deacon Greg Doud (gdoud@stcolumban.org) containing the responses for each parishioner.
