Instructions for assigning callers to candidates:

This page will assign the committee members to the parishioner candidates that need to be called and asked if they will participate in the upcoming discernment.

Any parishioner candidates that have been assigned callers manually will be skipped and the manually assigned committee caller will remain as it.

When the assignment of callers is complete, the parishioners list will display automatically.

When clicking the "Auto Assign Parishioners to Committee Callers", you'll be asked to verify your action before any changes are made.
